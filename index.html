We present Kamala Villa, a purchase loan opportunity, which consists in the granting of a loan to a developer to finance the purchase of a villa in Kamala Beach, Phuket, Thailand and then proceed to its management as guest house.

Reasons to Invest

in Kamala Villa

  • A project with a unique location very close to Patong, Phuket

  • Kamala Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Phuket. With its lagoon shape and shallow water it is a family friendly beach with many local restaurants and fun activities

  • The scoring of this opportunity is A

Location

The project is located in Kamala, Phuket, an elegant and town on the beautiful Phuket Island Coast in Thailand.

Highlights of the location:

  • It is the ideal holiday destination for those tourists who are looking for a pleasant and varied destination;
  • Kamala is a quiet and tranquil area on Phuket's west coast, north of Kalim beach and the lively Patong. It's an exclusive area with its own area known as 'Millionaire's Mile' named because of the luxury homes nestled in the hillsides overlooking the breathtaking Andaman Sea.
  • The project is located only 8 km from Patong.

The Project

The project consists of the purchase, renovation and a villa in Kamala Beach, Phuket. Thanks to a complete renovation, the developer wants to increase the value of the villa, in particular the renovation work planned:

  • Replacement of all window frames with new double-glazed with thermally cut;
  • Interior refurbishment: 3 bedrooms each with private bathroom, a new kitchen and refurbishment of the existing garage;
  • Complete indoor and outdoor painting;
  • Improvement of energy efficiency;
  • Improvement of the exterior environment: garden, construction of a new shading pergola.

The Developer

The development company is Onyx Investment Ltd. Is an German company dedicated to the remodelling of residential properties in the high and premium segment, in the coastal areas in Thailand and The Philippines.

It operates in parallel in the remodelling of real estate properties with the objective of long-term profitability.

With the headquarter in Munich, Onyx Investment Ltd. offers a specialized service in all aspects of real estate, from the development of opportunities, commercial analysis, acquisition, study of transformations to maximize added value, supervision of external construction, supervision of the work by experienced engineers, promotion and managing of guest houses, support of sales in the emerging South-East Asia.

Onyx Investment Ltd. is a company that has existed since 1994, with a long history in the rehabilitation segment and an experienced team in the real estate area. Onyx Investment Ltd, manages a portfolio of estates valued at 10 000 000 euro.
